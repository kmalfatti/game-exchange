About The Game Exchange

Never get ripped off again...

We know the feeling. You spent over $60 for that brand new game, played it for a few weeks, tried to trade it back in, and they will only offer you a quarter of what you paid.

Enter The Game Exchange: the worlds first platform where you can seemlessly trade video games with other players in your community!

Getting Started

  • Set up your profile
  • Add the games that you want to play to your profile.
  • Add the games that you own, and are willing to trade for the games that you want to play.

How it works

  • We will display a list of users in your area who are willing to trade games with you!
  • Send a message to a user and confirm a meetup stop and time that works for the both of you.
  • Log back in and confirm the trade has taken place and rate the other user.

Cashless Transaction

You only exchange games with people who are willing to trade straight-up for your game. No cash involved.

Rating System

The rating system helps ensure accountability for all users. If the game is not fully functional as it is suppose to be; if the other person is late to the meeting point, or if the person is excessively uncooperative, a user has the option anonymously to rate the other person 1 to 5 stars after each transqaction. A rating of 3 or less means they will never be show up on eachothers list of available traders again.
